🎓 Language School — Monday: Work & Careers
🇩🇪
Su lernt Deutsch
Arbeitstag
[AR-bits-tahg]
noun (m) — workday / working day
🗣 "Schöner Arbeitstag heute, oder?"
→ "Nice workday today, huh?"
💡 Memory trick: "Arbeit" = work + "Tag" = day. Think of it as your "orbit-tag" — the day you circle around work. Arbeitstag.
✨ Bonus: "Feierabend" — literally "celebration evening" = the moment work ends. The Germans made a whole word for quitting time. You love it already.

Alex aprende Português
Reunião
[he-OO-nyow]
noun (f) — meeting / gathering
🗣 "Tenho uma reunião importante hoje."
→ "I have an important meeting today."
💡 Memory trick: It sounds like "re-union" — and a meeting is everyone re-uniting in one room. Reunião.
✨ Bonus phrase: "Tô em reunião" = I'm in a meeting (casual/WhatsApp). You'll need this one daily. Also: "Reunião chata" = boring meeting. Very useful.

Monday Communication Tip
Pick one thing you want to tell the other person today — not about logistics, not about problems. Something you noticed or appreciated. Say it out loud, not in a text.

Monday has a way of turning people into task managers. Don't let it. One moment of real attention — "I noticed you did X and it meant something to me" — does more than a hundred good intentions. High performers forget this most.
Before the day ends: say one true thing out loud.
