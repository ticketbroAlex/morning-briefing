🎓 Language School — Tuesday: Food & Meals
🇩🇪
Su lernt Deutsch
Mittagessen
[MIT-tahg-es-sen]
noun (n) — lunch / midday meal
🗣 "Was gibt's heute zum Mittagessen?"
→ "What's for lunch today?"
💡 Memory trick: "Mittag" = midday + "Essen" = food/eating. Your midday food moment. Mittag-essen. Easy.
✨ Bonus: "Mittagspause" = lunch break. "Ich bin in der Mittagspause" = I'm on lunch break. Essential for WhatsApp.
🇧🇷
Alex aprende Português
Saudade
[saw-DAH-djee]
noun (f) — untranslatable. A warm, melancholic longing for someone or something you love.
🗣 "Tenho tanta saudade de você."
→ "I miss you so much." (but deeper than that)
💡 Memory trick: It's what you feel for someone you love who isn't there right now — not sadness, not just missing. Something beautiful and aching at once. Brazilians invented a word for it because they had to.
✨ Bonus: "Que saudade!" = I've missed this so much! / I've missed you! Use it when you see Su after a trip — she'll melt.
